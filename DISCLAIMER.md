# ⚠️ Disclaimer

## Not for Clinical Use

**Project Confluence is a computational research prototype.** It is NOT a medical device, clinical tool, or treatment recommendation system.

### What This Is

- A **hypothetical framework** for exploring cancer therapy optimization using mathematical models
- A **computational hypothesis generator** based on attractor dynamics and escape theory
- An **in silico proof-of-concept** that has NOT been validated in wet-lab experiments, animal models, or clinical trials

### What This Is NOT

- A validated clinical tool
- A substitute for medical advice
- A basis for treatment decisions
- A "cure" for cancer in any clinical sense

### Key Limitations

1. **No experimental validation** — All results are purely computational simulations
2. **Simplified biology** — 10-metabolite state space cannot capture the full complexity of tumor ecosystems
3. **Assumed drug additivity** — Real drug interactions are non-linear and dose/schedule-dependent
4. **Single-compartment immune model** — Does not capture T-cell trafficking or tissue-specific barriers
5. **Population-level generators** — Not calibrated to individual patient metabolomics

### Responsible Use

If you are a researcher using this framework:
- Treat all outputs as **hypotheses to be tested**, not conclusions
- Validate any computational predictions with appropriate experimental methods
- Follow all relevant ethical guidelines for computational biology research
- Do NOT use simulation results to inform clinical decisions
